This widget shows your documents. It shows the most recently created document first.

The following options can be configured in the widget's settings:

  • Title of Widget.
  • Display style. Show only the title or the title plus teaser text from each document.
  • How many documents to display.
  • Whether a pager should be displayed when more documents are available than can be shown at once.
  • Presence and label of a more link to direct visitors to additional documents.
  • Destination for above link.
