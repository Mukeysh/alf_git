You'll want to log in and set up some basic site information such as your name, professional title etc.

New Password, Name, Title and Photo

If this is the first time logging into your account, you'll be asked to confirm your email address and select a more permanent password; enter your name; your professional title and add a photo of yourself. (Name, title, and photograph are not required.)

Cropping your photo

You can upload a photo and crop it to the default size and shape by grabbing the cropping tool handles and adjusting to your preference. You'll see a preview of the cropped photo to the left of the cropping area. You'll want to make sure the original image you upload is at least the size of the default image (180px by 140px).

Once you've added your name, title and photo you're on your way to building your site.
